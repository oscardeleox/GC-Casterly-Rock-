What to Expect

A closer look at each step.

01

Fill out the form

~ 5 minutes

The form is broken into five sections so it doesn't feel overwhelming. We ask what we need to give you a real quote — nothing more.

  • Your contact info and how you'd like us to reach you (English, Spanish, or both)
  • A quick snapshot of your business — type, size, structure
  • What services you're looking for, even if you're not 100% sure
  • Where things stand today — what software, how current your books are
  • Your timeline, budget range, and biggest pain point
02

We review and prepare your quote

Within 24 hours

A real person at GC — not a chatbot — sits down with what you sent. We figure out exactly what you need, what we'd actually do, and what it should cost.

  • Custom pricing built for your situation — not a generic package
  • A clear list of what's included — no fine print, no surprises
  • Sent your way through the contact method you chose
03

Free 30-minute consultation

No commitment

Once you've seen the quote, we hop on a quick call to walk you through it. This is when you ask the hard questions.

  • Walk through the quote line by line so you know exactly what you're getting
  • Talk through your goals — and tell you honestly if we're the right fit
  • Conducted in English or Spanish — your choice
  • Only after the call do you decide whether to move forward. Zero pressure.
